Delete Your Account - Aquabuddy

If you would like to delete your Aquabuddy account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Send us an email at suyogshejal.dev@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Account
  3. Include the email address linked to your Aquabuddy account in the message.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your personal details (Name, Email, Gender, Age)
  • Health-related data (Height, Weight, Sleep Time, Wake Time, Water consumption)
  • Any other data stored in your account

Data Retention

- All your data will be deleted from our active databases within 30 days of your request.
- Backups may be retained for up to 90 days but will not be used for any purpose.
